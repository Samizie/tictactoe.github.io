ABOUT THE GAME

Tic-tac-toe, also known as noughts and crosses, is a classic paper-and-pencil game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3x3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game. There are several approaches to creating an AI player for tic-tac-toe, including the use of the minimax algorithm with alpha-beta pruning, TensorFlow.js for training a neural network, and the development of an unbeatable AI using the minimax algorithm. These approaches aim to create a computer player that provides a significant advantage over human players and can play a perfect game, never losing.

The algorithm used in this project is the Minimax Algorithm.

features

1. Text-Based Console Front End:
The game is built with a text-based console front end for human players, providing a simple and accessible interface for gameplay

2.Random and Unbeatable AI Player Options:
The first stage of the game involves a random player, which adds an element of unpredictability to the game and reduces the amount of computation required before running the more complex minimax algorithm then The game features an AI opponent that utilizes the minimax algorithm with alpha-beta pruning, ensuring that the AI player never loses and always makes the best move

In summary, the tic-tac-toe game with AI can offer a range of features, including a text-based console front end, unbeatable AI opponents, neural network training, and various approaches to solving the game, providing an engaging and challenging experience for players
